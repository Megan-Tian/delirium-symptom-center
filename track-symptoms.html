About CAM

This application utilizes the Confusion Assessment Method (CAM) test to track patients' symptoms and delirium "status". CAM is a standardized, efficient, and accurate procedure used to screen for delirium. Elderly patients should be frequently assessed to determine progression and severity. Read more about CAM here.

The CAM exam should be administed by a trained healthcare professional. Once the test is complete, upload the results into the form below. To view a full history of the patient's CAM results, go the symptom history page.
